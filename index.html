About Mauri

May 25, 1944 - December 11, 2020

Mauri lived life as a free spirit, passed of natural, pre-existing health conditions, and will be missed and treasured in memory by her brother Len, her niece Cynthia (and her husband Simon), her nephew John (and his wife Marilyn), her son Shaun (and his wife Julie), and by the abundance of people whose life she touched on a daily basis.

Site Information

This page is the start of a memorial and celebration site dedicated to a much-loved sister, aunt, and mother, made with love to preserve and enrich the memory of our beloved Maurita.
